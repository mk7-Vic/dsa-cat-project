Queue Operations

Enqueue: Every newly registered parcel is added to the end of the dispatch queue.

Dequeue: When the dispatch button is clicked, the parcel at the front of the queue is dispatched first.

Queue Principle: First In, First Out (FIFO)

The parcel at Queue Position #1 is always dispatched before every other parcel.
